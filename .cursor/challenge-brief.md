# Pyramid Backstage
by Albanian-American Development Foundation (AADF) | Albania

## The Challenge
Reimagining the invisible work behind every event at the Pyramid of Tirana.

## Problem
Today, event coordination at the Pyramid happens through emails, phone calls, Excel spreadsheets, and institutional memory. A request like "I'd like to organize a startup conference for 180 people next month" triggers hours of back-and-forth. Without a single source of truth, valuable time is spent coordinating logistics instead of creating exceptional experiences.

## Mission
Design a solution that transforms an event request into an event-ready operational plan. Help the Pyramid answer instantly: "can we make this happen?" — and if yes, "what's next?".

## Core requirements
- Receive and manage event requests in one place
- Match requests with appropriate Pyramid spaces
- Check availability across venues and dates
- Automatically generate quotations and proposals
- Track inventory of operational assets (chairs, tables, microphones, screens, equipment)
- Know where assets are located and how many remain available
- Reserve assets for confirmed events
- Detect scheduling and resource conflicts
- Generate setup and teardown task lists
- Coordinate responsibilities across operational teams
- Maintain a complete record of decisions, changes, and approvals

## Spaces
Four main event spaces on floors 0 and -1 (Blue, Orange, Green, Yellow) host most activities, but events spill into entrance, corridors, and informal areas. The MVRDV-renovated Pyramid (2023) also includes Roof L+4, 3rd Floor, and 16 Exterior Boxes.
